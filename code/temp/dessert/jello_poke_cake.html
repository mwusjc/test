Jell-O Poke Cake


Prepare one 2 layer size cake mix as directed on package. Cool in pans for 15 minutes. Set aside, do not remove from pans.

Dissolve 2 packages (85g) each, Orange Jelly Powder (or any flavour) in 2 cups (500ml) boiling water, stirring until clear.

Poke cake layers all over tops with a large fork or wooden skewer. Pour half the jelly over each cake layer in pan. Chill for 4 hours.

Unmould one cake layer, round side down, onto serving plate. Spread with 1 cup (250ml) thawed Cool Whip Whipped Topping from 1lt size container.

Place second layer on top, round side up. Frost top and sides of cake with additional Cool Whip Whipped Topping. Garnish as desired.

Chill for at least 1 hour before serving. Can be made a day ahead and stored in refrigerator.
