Image uploading is the one thing you must remember. 

Your website is not configured to upload images to the server automatically.  This means that your images will have to be already on the internet if you are copying and pasting from a Word or other file on your computer.  Images can easily be copy and pasted if you see them on the internet anywhere already..

i.e. select an image you see on our main page and right click and copy it.  Then you can paste into your input area to submit your materials.
