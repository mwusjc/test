Egg Noodle Soup with Vegetables

1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 medium onion, chopped
2 cups chopped zucchini
2 cups chopped carrots
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1/3 cup fresh basil
2 medium tomatoes, chopped
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 teaspoon parsley
2 (14-oz) cans chicken broth
1 (9-oz) pkg. egg noodles
2 cups frozen corn kernels
Grated Parmesan Cheese


In a 5-quart soup kettle, heat oil until hot over medium heat, Add onion, zucchini, carrots and garlic. Cook, stirring often, until onion is limp, about 5 minutes. Add basil, tomatoes, peppers, parsley and broth. Heat to boiling about 10 minutes. Stir in noodles and corn. Reduce heat to medium and simmer until noodles are tender, 5 to 6 minutes. Serve in bowls topped with a sprinkling of grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
