Rice Stuffing

1/4 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup chopped celery
2 tablespoons butter or margarine
1 cup long-grain white uncooked rice
2 chicken-flavoured bouillon cubes
2 cups hot water
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon poultry seasoning


Cook onion and celery in butter in frypan until tender. Add rice; heat, stirring often, about 5 minutes. Add remaining ingredients; bring to boil. Reduce heat; cover tightly. Simmer about 15 minutes, until rice is tender and all liquid absorbed. Easy and delicious stuffing for your Easter turkey.
