Fish Fillets with Basil Butter

1 pound ocean perch fillets
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 tablespoon melted butter
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
salt and pepper to taste


Arrange fillets in a single layer in a baking dish. Mix lemon juice, melted butter and basil together. Drizzle over fish. Sprinkle lightly with salt and pepper. Bake, uncovered, in pre-heated 450°F oven for 8 - 10 minutes or until fish is opaque.
