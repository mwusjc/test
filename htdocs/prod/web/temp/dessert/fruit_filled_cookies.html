Fruit Filled Cookies

1 package (18oz.) refrigerated sugar cookie dough
1/4 cup raspberry jam or jelly


Preheat oven to 350º. Divide dough in half. Using serrated knife, cut one half of dough into 1/8-inch slices, about 24. Refrigerate remaining half of dough. Place slices 2 inches apart on ungreased baking sheets. Spread each slice with a 1/2 teaspoon of jam, leaving a 1/4-inch edge around each cookie. Set aside. Using a serrated knife, cut reserved dough into 1/8-inch slices, about 24. Using a 1-inch star-shaped cookie cutter, cut out a star from the center of each cookie slice. Set stars aside for baking (see tip) or to reroll. Place the cutout slices on top of jam-filled cookie slices; press edges together slightly. Bake 10 to 14 minutes, or until edges are light golden brown. Cool 1 minute on baking sheets, then transfer to wire racks to cool completely.

Tip: To bake tiny stars, place on ungreased baking sheet and bake at 350º for 4 to 6 minutes.
