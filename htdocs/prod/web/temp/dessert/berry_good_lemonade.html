Berry Good Lemonade

1 cup hulled, sliced strawberries
1 cup of raspberries
3/4 cup plus 1 tablespoon sugar, depending on the sweetness of the fruit
1-1/2 cups lemon juice (4-5 large lemons)


Place berries and sugar in a food processor and puree until liquid. Strain through a fine sieve, stirring with a spatula. Pour strained juice into a large pitcher. Add lemon juice and 1-3/4 cups water. Chill. Stir well before serving in tall, ice-filled glasses.
