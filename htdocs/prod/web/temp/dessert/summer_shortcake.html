Summer Fruit Shortcake

FILLING/SAUCE
3 cups mixed fresh fruit (blueberries, strawberries, raspberries, blackberries, etc.)
3 tablespoons granulated sugar
1 tbsp lemon juice
1 cup strawberry puree
1 cup whipping cream, whipped


LEMON BISCUITS
Milk
grated rind and juice of 1 lemon
2 cups all purpose flour
2 tbsp. granulated sugar
4 tsp. baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons shortening
2 tablespoons butter


In bowl, sprinkle fruit with 2 tbsp. of sugar & lemon juice; toss to mix. Set aside. Lemon Biscuits: In measuring cup, add enough milk to lemon juice to make 2/3 cup; stir, set aside for 15 mins to sour. In bowl, stir together flour, sugar, baking powder, salt & lemon rind; using pastry blender or two knives, cut in shortening & butter until well mixed. With a fork, blend in soured milk to make soft dough. Turn out dough onto lightly floured surface; knead 6 to 8 times. Roll out or pat dough to 1/2" thickness. Using floured 3" round cutter, cut out 6 biscuits. Sprinkle lightly with more sugar. Bake on greased baking sheet in 425°F oven for 12 to 15 mins or until browned. Let cool until warm; cut into half horizontally. Spoon fruit mixture onto bottom halves of biscuits, reserving some fruit for garnish. Stir strawberry puree with remaining sugar; spoon over fruit mixture. Top with dollop of whipped cream and biscuit tops. Garnish with more cream & remaining fruit.
