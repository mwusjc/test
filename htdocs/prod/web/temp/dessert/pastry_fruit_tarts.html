Pastry Fruit Tarts

1 package pie crust mix
1 egg, beaten
1 to 2 tablespoons cold water
1-1/2 cups half & half milk
1 package instant pudding mix (vanilla)
Fresh berries, sliced
mandarin oranges,
strawberries, peaches
& kiwi fruit


Preheat oven to 425°F. Combine pie crust mix with egg. Add just enough water to form dough. Form dough rounds and press inside small tart pans. Pierce pastry with fork. Place on baking sheet & bake for 10 mins. Cool completely & remove from tins. Beat pudding mix and half & half milk well. Spoon into shells and arrange fresh fruit slices on pudding. Chill & serve.
