Spicy Sausage & Greens Pasta

1/2 cup pure olive oil
2 leeks, white and pale green parts only, chopped
1/4 teaspoon salt
3/4 pound hot Italian sausage, sliced
1/2 cup chicken stock
1/2 teaspoon hot pepper flakes
6 cups coarsely chopped dandelion greens
3/4 pound rotini
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese


In a large saucepan, heat olive oil over medium-low heat. Add leeks and salt, cover and cook 10 minutes, or until softened. Increase heat to medium; add sausage and cook 7 minutes, or until browned. Stir in chicken stock and hot pepper flakes. Add greens; cook 3 minutes or just until greens wilt, turning frequently. In a large pot of boiling, salted water, cook pasta 8-10 minutes, or until tender but firm; drain. Toss pasta with sausage and greens. Sprinkle with parmesan cheese and serve immediately.
TIP: Spinach or Swiss chard may be substituted for dandelion greens.
