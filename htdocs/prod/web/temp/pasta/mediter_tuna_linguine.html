Mediterranean Tuna Linguine

8 ounces uncooked linguine
1 can chunk light tuna in water
1 tablespoon olive oil or vegetable oil
1/4 cup finely chopped celery
3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
1 can crushed tomatoes undrained
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
1/3 cup quartered pitted Kalamata olives
Fresh Italian sprigs, parsley


Cook and drain linguine as directed on package. While linguine is cooking, heat oil in 10" skillet over medium-high heat. Add celery; cook 2 to 3 minutes, stirring frequently, until celery begins to soften. Add garlic; cook and stir 30 seconds. Stir in tomatoes, tuna and pepper. Cook 5 to 8 minutes, stirring frequently, until slightly thickened. Stir in olives. Cook 2 to 3 minutes or until hot. Serve sauce over linguine. Garnish with parsley.
