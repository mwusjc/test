Four Cheese Manicotti

12 uncooked manicotti
Vegetable cooking spray
1/2 cup finely chopped onion
3 garlic cloves, minced
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese; divided
1/2 cup grated fresh Parmesan cheese, divided
1 teaspoon dried Italian seasoning
1/2 teaspoon of pepper
1 (15 ounce) carton ricotta cheese
1 (6 ounce) package garden vegetable-flavoured cream cheese, softened
4 ounces block nonfat cream cheese, softened
1/2 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed, drained, and squeezed dry
1 jar reduced-fat, reduced-sodium tomato-and-herb pasta sauce Oregano sprigs


Cook pasta according to package. Coat a small nonstick skillet with cooking spray and place over medium-high heat, add onion and garlic. Saute for 3 minutes. Remove and set aside. Combine 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, and the next 5 ingredients in a bowl; beat at medium speed of a mixer until smooth. Stir in onion mixture and spinach. Spoon mozzarella cheese mixture into cooked manicotti (about 1/3 cup per shell). Arrange manicotti in a dish coated with cooking spray. Pour tomato sauce over manicotti. Cover baking dish with foil, and bake at 350°F for 25 minutes. Sprinkle with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses; bake, uncovered, an additional 5 minutes. Garnish with oregano, if desired. (Serving size: 2 manicotti each).
