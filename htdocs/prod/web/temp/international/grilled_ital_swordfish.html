Grilled Italian Swordfish

6 (6oz) swordfish steaks
3 tablespoons lime juice
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 tablespoons sour cream
2 tablespoons granulated sugar
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon oregano
1/2 teaspoon thyme leaves
1/2 teaspoon cracked black pepper
1/2 crushed red chilli peppers


Wash the steaks and pat dry. Place in a shallow pan. In another bowl, combine the lime juice, oil, sour cream, sugar, salt, oregano, thyme leaves, black pepper and chilli pepper. Mix ingredients well. Pour the mixture over the swordfish steaks and allow to marinate for at least four hours. Grill steaks on the barbecue for five minutes per side, while continuously brushing with marinade. Serve swordfish with potato wedges or a variety of fresh steamed or grilled vegetables.
