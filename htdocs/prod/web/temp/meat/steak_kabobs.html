Steak Kabobs

2 tablespoons reduced-sodium soy sauce
2 tablespoons honey
1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger or
1 teaspoon ground ginger
1 clove garlic, crushed
1 teaspoon grated lemon peel
1/4 teaspoon crushed hot red pepper flakes
12 ounces boneless sirloin steak, trimmed
and cut into 1" cubes
8 cherry tomatoes
4 large mushrooms, cut in half
1 green bell pepper, cored, seeded, and cut into 8 squares


In a shallow glass dish, combine soy sauce, honey, ginger, garlic, lemon peel, and red pepper flakes. Mix well. Add beef; stir to coat. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 1 to 2 hours, stirring occasionally. Preheat broiler. Remove beef from the marinade. Using four 10" metal skewers, alternately thread beef, tomatoes, mushrooms, and bell pepper. Place on hot grill. Grill 2 or 3 times until meat is medium-rare and vegetables browned, about 10 minutes. Place on serving plates and serve immediately.

TIP: Choose vegetables with similar cooking times, then cut them into bite-size pieces or lengths. Small vegetables like cherry tomatoes are best kept whole. Green onions, fennel, red onion, eggplant, and summer squash would also be appropriate vegetables for skewering. For best results, choose skewers that are square or flat; they will hold food more securely.
