The Rexall Reminder Ready™ Service:

  • Is a free service
  • Automatically renews your prescription
  • Effortlessly manages your prescription medications
  • Ensures your prescription renewal is ready when you need it

How it works

  • Notifies our expert pharmacists four days before your refill is due
  • Allows our pharmacists to contact your doctor directly if you have no refills*
  • Calls you at your home, work or mobile number to let you know when your prescriptions are ready to
    pick up
  • Enrol in-store or over the phone

*Doctor permitting. Any changes to prescription, including changes in dosage, should be reported to your pharmacy.

 

Automatic Prescription Renewal

When you get your prescriptions filled at a Rexall™ Pharma Plus, you can enrol in Rexall Reminder Ready™ service. This service notifies our expert pharmacists of your prescription renewal four days before your refill is due. Then we’ll call you when your medications are ready to be picked up. No more missed refills, no more waiting, one less thing to remember!
